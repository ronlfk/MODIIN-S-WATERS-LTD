Privacy Policy for Our Games

Last updated: 24 Nov 2025

We develop and publish mobile games. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you play our games or use any related services (the “Games” or “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Games, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system, and language)
  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location (country or region level)
  • App usage data, ad interactions, crash reports, and performance data

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

2. Game Progress & Local Data

Game progress, preferences, and settings are primarily stored locally on your device. Some non-personal and aggregated data may be collected to improve app stability and performance.

3. Ads, Analytics, and Third-Party SDKs

Our Games use third-party services, including Google AdMob, to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and process data such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location

This data is used to display ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. Google’s data practices are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4. In-App Purchases

If a Game offers optional in-app purchases, payments are processed by the app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not collect or store payment or credit card details.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Games may be used by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Where required by law, ads may be shown as non-personalized.

6. Data Retention

Collected data is retained only as long as necessary to operate and improve our Games, comply with legal obligations, and support advertising services. Local game data remains on your device until you delete the Game or clear its storage.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including the right to request access to or deletion of your data and to opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
