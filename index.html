Privacy Policy for Our Games

Last updated: 24 Nov 2025

MODIIN'S WATERS LTD (“we”, “our”, “us”) develops and publishes mobile games. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you play our games or use any related services (the “Games” or “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or user-identifiable data through our Games.

This means we do not collect things like:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or account details
  • Precise location
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or tracking IDs
  • Contacts, photos, files, camera, or microphone data
  • Payment information
  • Gameplay analytics tied to a specific user

2. Game Progress & Local Data

Any progress or settings are stored locally on your device only (for example: level completion, in-game preferences). We do not receive or upload this data to our servers.

3. Ads, Analytics, and SDKs

Our Games do not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not show ads, do not run behavioral analytics, and do not share data with advertisers.

If we ever add such services in the future, we will update this policy before the change takes effect.

4. In-App Purchases

If a Game offers optional purchases, the transaction is processed by the app store (Google Play / Apple App Store). We do not collect or store payment details. The store may handle purchase data under its own privacy policy.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Games may be used by children. Because we do not collect personal data, no information is knowingly collected from children under 13 (or any age).

6. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data because none is collected. Local game data remains on your device until you delete the Game or clear its storage.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data for you to access, correct, or delete from our systems. If you have concerns, you can contact us anytime.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements to our Games or legal requirements. The updated version will be published on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
